Vegan spaghetti

You need: spaghetti, tomato sauce, onions, soja granulate, garlic, oregano

Ingredients

  • 500 ml Tomato sauce
  • 500 gSpaghetti
  • 3 garlic toes
  • 50 g soja granulate
  • 1 onion

Steps

  1. Cut the onions and the garlic in little pieces
  2. Grill the onions and garlic in a pan with oil
  3. Add the tomato sauce and the soja granules to the pan
  4. Cook 1 l of water and add salt
  5. Add the spaghetti to the cooking water and cook for 10 minutes
  6. Test if the spaghetti are "al dente"
