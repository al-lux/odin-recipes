Pancakes

Good old pancakes

You need: eggs, milk, flour, salt, water

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 200 g flour
  • 100 ml whole milk
  • 50 ml water
  • half a teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Put the flour and the salt in a bowl
  2. Add the eggs, milk, and water
  3. Squirl the ingredients into an homogenous mass
  4. Heat oil in a pan on medium temperature
  5. Give the dough in the pan and bake for 3 minutes then flip the pancake
